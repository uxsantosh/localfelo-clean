# Email to Interakt Support - Template Permission Issue

## Subject: Unable to Create Templates - Need Guidance

---

## Email Template:

```
To: support@interakt.shop
Subject: Unable to Create Templates in Meta - Need Guidance

Hi Interakt Team,

I am building a WhatsApp notification system for my app LocalFelo using Interakt's API.

When I try to create message templates in Meta's WhatsApp Manager, I get this error:

"This WhatsApp Business account does not have permission to create message template"

My Questions:

1. Should I create templates in Interakt Dashboard instead of Meta directly?
2. Do I need Production/Live access to create custom templates?
3. Am I currently in Sandbox/Test mode?
4. What steps do I need to take to create the following templates?
   - Authentication template (OTP verification)
   - Marketing templates (task notifications, listing alerts, etc.)

My Account Details:
- WhatsApp Number: [YOUR WHATSAPP NUMBER]
- Business Name: LocalFelo
- Use Case: Hyperlocal marketplace (OTP, task notifications, chat alerts)

Please guide me on:
- How to create templates (Interakt Dashboard vs Meta)
- Whether I need to upgrade to Production access
- Required business verification steps (if any)

Thank you for your help!

Best regards,
[Your Name]
```

---

## Alternative: Quick Support Chat

If Interakt has live chat:

1. Login to Interakt Dashboard
2. Click support chat icon (usually bottom right)
3. Type: "I'm getting 'no permission to create template' error in Meta. Should I create templates in Interakt Dashboard instead?"
4. Follow their instructions

---

## Expected Response

Interakt will likely tell you:

**Option 1: Use Interakt Dashboard**
"Yes, please create templates in Interakt Dashboard. We'll submit to Meta for approval on your behalf."

**Option 2: Upgrade Required**
"You're currently in Sandbox mode. To create custom templates, you need Production access. Here's how to apply..."

**Option 3: Verification Needed**
"Please complete business verification first. Here are the required documents..."

---

## What to Ask For

1. **Confirmation:** Can I create templates in Interakt Dashboard?
2. **Access Level:** Am I in Sandbox or Production mode?
3. **Template Limit:** How many templates can I create in my current plan?
4. **Approval Time:** How long for template approval (typically)?
5. **Production Upgrade:** What's required to get Production access?
6. **Testing:** Can I test templates before approval?

---

## Important Notes

- Interakt support is usually very responsive (4-24 hours)
- They prefer you create templates in their dashboard
- This gives them better control over quality and approval rates
- Your templates will still go to Meta for approval, but through Interakt
