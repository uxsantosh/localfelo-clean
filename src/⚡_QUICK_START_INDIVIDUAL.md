## 🚀 3 Steps to Get WhatsApp Working

### **STEP 1: Create Facebook Business Manager** (30 min)

**Do this NOW:**

1. Go to [business.facebook.com](https://business.facebook.com)
2. Click **"Create Account"**
3. Use your **personal information:**
   - Your personal name (NOT company name)
   - Personal email
   - Personal Facebook account
4. Complete setup

✅ **Done!** Keep this tab open.

---

### **STEP 2: Contact Interakt Support** (5 min)

**Send this message via Interakt chat:**

```
Hi Interakt Team,

I'm getting "Cannot create message template" error.

IMPORTANT QUESTION: Do you support INDIVIDUAL DEVELOPERS 
without business registration?

I'm an individual building a marketplace app. I have:
- Personal PAN Card ✅
- Aadhaar Card ✅  
- Address proof ✅
- Personal Facebook Business Manager ✅
- NO business registration (individual developer)

Can I get Production WhatsApp API access as an individual 
with just personal documents?

If YES: Please guide me through individual verification
If NO: I'll switch to Aisensy (they support individuals)

Thank you!
[Your Name]
[Email]  
[Phone]
```

**Where to send:**
- Interakt Dashboard → Chat icon (bottom right)
- OR email: support@interakt.ai

**Wait for response** (1-24 hours)

---

### **STEP 3A: If Interakt Says YES** (Continue with them)

**They'll guide you to:**

1. Upload documents:
   - PAN Card
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Address proof

2. Connect Facebook Business Manager

3. Wait for Meta approval (3-7 days)

4. Get Production access ✅

5. Create templates

6. Start sending WhatsApp! 🎉

---

### **STEP 3B: If Interakt Says NO** (Switch to Aisensy)

**Aisensy explicitly supports individuals!**

1. Go to [aisensy.com](https://aisensy.com/)
2. Click **"Get Started"**
3. Choose **"Free Plan"** (1,000 messages/month FREE)
4. Fill personal details:
   - Personal name
   - Personal email
   - Business type: "Individual"
5. Upload documents:
   - PAN Card
   - Aadhaar Card
   - Address proof
6. Connect Facebook Business Manager
7. Wait for approval (2-5 days)
8. Start sending WhatsApp! 🎉
