## ❓ Common Questions

### **Q: Why can't I create templates now?**
**A:** You need Production API access from Meta. This requires verification of your identity (PAN + Aadhaar) and takes 7-10 days.

### **Q: Can I use WhatsApp without business registration?**
**A:** YES! As an individual developer, you just need personal PAN + Aadhaar + Address proof.

### **Q: Why not use SMS instead?**
**A:** Good catch! SMS in India requires DLT registration and business documents. WhatsApp is actually easier for individuals!

### **Q: Which provider is best - Interakt or Aisensy?**
**A:** 
- **Interakt:** You already have account, try them first
- **Aisensy:** Explicitly supports individuals, easier approval, free tier
- **Recommendation:** Ask Interakt first. If they say NO to individuals, switch to Aisensy.

### **Q: How much does it cost?**
**A:** 
- **Aisensy:** First 1,000 conversations/month FREE, then ₹0.25-0.50 each
- **Interakt:** Similar, ask them
- **WhatsApp/Meta:** FREE to send messages (no per-message cost)

### **Q: What if I register business later?**
**A:** Easy upgrade! Just submit new business documents, keep all templates and setup.

### **Q: Can I test WhatsApp before approval?**
**A:** No external service works without approval. BUT your in-app notifications are already working! Use those for now.
